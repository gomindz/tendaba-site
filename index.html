About Us

Welcome to Tendaba

Tendaba is a unique town located on the bank of The River Gambia in West Africa. The town has a rich History that dates back to it's roots as a hunting camp. Overtime, Tendaba has evolved into an ecotourism destination with the tendaba camp, which offers visitors the chance to explore the nearby wetlands and savana and see a variety of bird species, including rare and endangered ones.

Tendaba is also home to several traditional griot families, who are highly respected in the community and play an essential role in preserving local culture. Visitors to tendaba can take a village tour using safari vehicles to learn about the towns customs and traditions. The town's history, culture and natural beauty makes it an incredible destination for anyone looking to expore West Africa.
